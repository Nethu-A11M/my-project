St. Paul's Girls' School Milagiriya

St. Paul's Girls' School, Milagiriya is one of the most respected girls’ schools in Colombo, widely admired for its long-standing traditions, academic excellence, and disciplined school culture. Over the years, the school has built a strong reputation for nurturing confident, independent, and capable young women who excel both in studies and in extracurricular activities. SPM places great emphasis on leadership, character development, and service, encouraging students to take responsibility, think critically, and contribute positively to society. The school offers a balanced education that supports academics alongside sports, clubs, societies, music, and community work. With a proud heritage and a forward-looking vision, St. Paul’s Girls’ School continues to shape generations of students who uphold strong moral values, resilience, and a sense of unity, while preparing them to face modern challenges with confidence and grace.

Past principles

  1. Mrs. Geetha Abegunawardana (2004 – 2014) — served before Dr. Jayaweera and helped shape the school’s growth into the 2010s.
  2. Dr. Sumedha Jayaweera (2015 – 2023) — dedicated many years to improving academics and school growth.
  3. Mrs. Chandhi Siriwardhana (Dec 2023 – 2024) — served as Acting Principal, contributing to the school’s progress.
  4. Mrs. Sureka Siriwardhana (July 26, 2024 – Dec 31, 2024) — continued leadership through 2024.
  5. Mrs. Nayomi Dassanayake (2025 – Present) — the current principal guiding ongoing development.
